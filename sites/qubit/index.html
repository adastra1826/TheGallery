research

Four programs, one question: what survives the noise?

Every group at QUBIT works the same seam from a different face — protecting quantum information long enough to do something irreversible with it.

EC

Error-corrected logic

Surface and bivariate-bicycle codes running live on fluxonium arrays. Two hundred sixteen physical qubits per logical qubit today — and falling every quarter.

Lead — Dr. Yusuf Adeyemi

TOP

Topological matter

Majorana zero modes in proximitized indium-antimonide nanowires: qubits that store information nowhere in particular, so the noise cannot find it.

Lead — Dr. Ingrid Solheim

NET

Quantum networks

Heralded entanglement over 41 km of dark fiber between Boulder and Longmont, at 1.1 kHz. A repeater you can drive to in twenty minutes.

Lead — Dr. Priya Raghunathan

ALG

Algorithms & complexity

Where does quantum advantage actually live? Lattice chemistry, sampling, and the honest end-to-end accounting of every claimed speedup.

Lead — Dr. Tomáš Krejčí
