THE LABEL

PRESSED AFTER DARK

Nightdrive Records started in October 2021 in a two-car garage off Huntington Drive in Duarte, California — one Juno-60 with a sticky G key, one four-track, one busted VCR used as a mastering reference. We press music for the hour when the interstate empties out and the radio starts telling the truth.

Every release ships on chrome cassette and translucent 12-inch vinyl, mastered loud enough to survive factory car speakers. Mixed for the driver's seat, not the listening room. If a track doesn't work at 88 miles per hour with the windows down, it doesn't come out.
